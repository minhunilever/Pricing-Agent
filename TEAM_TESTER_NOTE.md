# Pricing AI Strategist - Tester Note

## What To Test

Open the hosted app URL in Edge or Chrome, sign in with your company account, and upload the Stackline first-mover workbook.

Please test:

- Workbook loads successfully.
- Cockpit filters work for brand, retailer, channel, severity, and audience.
- Guardrail flags look directionally correct.
- Recommendations are understandable for RGM review.
- Legal review triggers feel appropriate.
- Ask Strategist answers are useful and do not overclaim margin or volume impact.
- CSV and executive brief exports work.

## Important

This is a decision-support prototype. It does not execute pricing changes and does not send retailer communication.

Every recommendation requires RGM approval before action.
